Syntax:
`:<uuid> FIDENT <new ident>`

Indicates that ident of `<source>` has changed to `<new ident>`.

Note: you CANNOT use this command to change the ident of users other than your own. To do that, you need to issue a `CHGIDENT`.

Source:
Always UUID

Routing:
Broadcast
