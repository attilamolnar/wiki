Syntax:
`:<uuid> NICK <new nickname> <timestamp>`

Indicates that the nickname of `<uuid>` has changed to `<new nickname>` at `<timestamp>`.
See Nick Collisions to see how nick collisions are handled. (TODO: link)

Source:
Always UUID

Routing:
Broadcast
