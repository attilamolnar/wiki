Syntax:
`:<uuid> FHOST <new host>`

Indicates that the displayed host of `<source>` has changed to `<new host>`.

Note: you CANNOT use this command to change the host of users other than your own. To do that, you need to issue a `CHGHOST`.

Source:
Always UUID

Routing:
Broadcast
